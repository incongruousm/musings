🍺 12 Belgian Birthday Beers of 2024

Jan 3, 2025

A little summary after working my way through 12 Belgian beers gifted to me for my birthday. Largely it’s been an enjoyable selection with just a couple of weak offerings. Mostly, these were all new to me. The Jupiler and Duvel I’ve seen everywhere but never tried and it’s easy to see why they’re popular. The St. Barnadus and Westmalle are real discoveries and I’m really happy to have found them - they’ll be reappearing in my future for sure!
